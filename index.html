Global Wealth Distribution Visualization

Explore how global wealth is distributed across custom wealth brackets. Define your own brackets by minimum wealth per adult (USD) to see what percentage of the world's adult population falls into each bracket and how much of total global wealth they hold.

Data source: UBS Global Wealth Report 2023 (2022 data)

The dataset aggregates wealth distribution from the UBS/Credit Suisse Global Wealth Report, which tracks household wealth across 200+ countries. Data includes 3.77 billion adults with tracked wealth data (out of ~5.4 billion global adults). The discrepancy exists because comprehensive wealth data is not available for all countries and populations.
